Canvas Games

Using Javascript for game logic and Canvas to render it, I have recreated some simple games

Snake

Use the arrow keys to move your snake and eat apples, carefully managing your snake's size

Minesweeper

Using math related clues, try to find all the bombs without setting any off!

Helicopter Game (an old flash game)

Control your altitude with the mouse to avoid crashing into the ceiling, floor, and obstacles

Chess

A 'pass and play' style of Chess. Use strategy to capture your opponent's King! (No I did not make a chess engine lol)
